Cards are a self-contained bit of information.

On this site, the card layout is used either in listings of resources or to contain lists of related resources.

~~~
<div class="card">
  <header class="header">
    <h3>
      <a rel="bookmark">Brace Yourself: OSLC Steering Committee Elections Are Coming</a>
    </h3>
  </header>
  <section class="content">
    <div class="copy">
      <p>[Content goes here]</p>
    </div>
  </section>
  <div class="footer">
    <footer class="meta">
      <span class="byline">
        <time datetime="Fri Apr 11 2014 00:00:00 GMT-0400 (EDT)">
          2014 April 11 
        </time>
        by <a>Sean Kennedy</a>
      </span>
    </footer>
  </div>
</div>
~~~
