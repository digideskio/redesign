[Sample resource] OSLC Community Update and An Introduction to PROMCODE

2014 February 19
Presentation

Sean Kennedy will provide a quick update on the status of the OSLC Community, including the new Technical Committees at OASIS and the upcoming OSLC Connect event at the ALM Forum. Next, Dr. Mikio Aoyama will introduce to a new OSLC effort called PROMCODE (PROject Management for COntracted DElivery), which is on Open Platform for Contracted Software Development Management of Software Supply Chains.
